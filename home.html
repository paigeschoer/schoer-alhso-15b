Welcome to P&B Sweets!

P&B Sweets is a bakery located in Wabasso, MN. At this bakery we make all kinds of sweets and goodies! We have everything from cookies to wedding cakes but we LOVE making our special cupcakes! We make all different kinds of flavors of cupcakes and try new recipes and ideas to make all of our cupcakes special.

You can either come to the store an dorder in person, or you can order our baked goods online and we will ship them to you when you want them. We never want you to worry about getting the right cakes or goods for any event, so we will do our best in giving you what you want.
